AI Projects

As a product and technology adviser for a tech-for-good startup, I evaluate and implement solutions that align technical capabilities with organisational needs and constraints.

My approach combines strategic assessment with practical implementation. For operational requirements such as customer relationship management, I recommended HubSpot's free tier, which provided immediate value whilst integrating seamlessly with existing infrastructure and minimal setup overhead.

For more complex requirements, I conducted build-versus-buy analyses that considered budget constraints, timeline requirements, and long-term maintainability. Where commercial solutions proved cost-prohibitive or inflexible, I developed custom AI-powered automation tools designed to integrate directly into existing workflows with minimal disruption.

The projects showcased below represent this pragmatic approach to technology implementation: solving real problems with appropriate tools, whether that means configuring existing platforms or building bespoke solutions.

Automated CV Screening System

Overview: An AI-powered recruitment automation system that reduces manual CV screening time by 90% whilst maintaining consistent evaluation criteria.

Small businesses and startups often lack dedicated HR resources, spending hours manually reviewing applications without standardised assessment frameworks. This system addresses the inefficiency by automatically extracting, analysing, and scoring job applications against customisable criteria, providing evidence-based recommendations with supporting quotations from each CV.

Technologies: Make.com, Claude AI, Google Workspace APIs

Key Features:

  • Automated document processing
  • AI-powered candidate analysis
  • Evidence-based scoring
  • 90% time reduction in screening
