About Me

Hi, I’m Ashwini, a Product Manager focused on solving real user problems and driving measurable business outcomes.

I believe great product management starts with deep empathy for users, understanding their pain points and translating insights into solutions. I use both qualitative and quantitative data to inform decisions.

My approach is experimental: I validate hypotheses early, test frequently, and learn fast. Whether at the 0→1 stage or scaling established products, I treat each initiative like a startup, balancing vision, validation, and delivery discipline.

Before moving into product management, I worked as a software engineer. I still like to build using no-code and low-code tools, and I experiment with AI-assisted IDEs such as Windsurf.
