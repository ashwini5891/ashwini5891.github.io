About Me

Hi, I’m Ashwini, a Product Manager passionate about building products that solve real user problems and drive measurable business outcomes.

I believe great product management starts with deep empathy for users, understanding their pain points and translating those insights into impactful solutions. I rely on both qualitative and quantitative data to inform decisions, ensuring every product choice is grounded in evidence, not assumptions.

My approach is highly experimental: I validate hypotheses early, test frequently, and learn fast. Whether at the 0→1 stage or scaling established products, I treat every initiative like a startup, balancing vision, validation, and delivery discipline.

Before moving into product management, I worked as a software engineer. I still love to build — whether using no-code and low-code tools or experimenting with AI-assisted IDEs like Windsurf. This hands-on mindset helps me stay close to the technical craft and collaborate effectively with engineers.
