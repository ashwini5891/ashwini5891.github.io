About

Hi, I am Ashwini, a Product Manager focused on creating meaningful user value and measurable business outcomes.

Whilst my professional experience centres on B2B SaaS, I actively explore B2C products through side ventures whilst developing AI-powered automation solutions and championing PLG/PLS strategies that are reshaping modern product development.

AI Projects → Curated PLG/PLS Posts →

I believe great product management starts with deep empathy for users, understand the challenges they face, and turn insights into solutions. I use both qualitative and quantitative data to inform decisions.

My approach is experimental. I validate hypotheses early, test frequently, and learn fast. Whether at the 0 to 1 stage or scaling established products, I treat each initiative like a startup, balancing vision, validation, and delivery discipline.

Before product management, I worked as a software engineer. I still like to build using no-code, low-code and AI-assisted tools. I experiment with AI-driven workflows, model evals, and integration prototypes to explore how automation and reasoning tools can speed up product work.
